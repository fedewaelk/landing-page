Get to know our basic ingredients

Malt is the heart of beer. Made from barley or other grains, it imparts rich flavors, from sweet and toasty to caramel and nutty. The type of malt used shapes the beer’s color, texture, and body.

Hops are the flowers that give beer its distinct aroma and bitterness. They balance the sweetness of the malt and contribute floral, citrus, pine, or earthy flavors.

Water is the most essential ingredient in beer. The mineral content in water affects the beer’s taste, and different brewing regions use water with varying characteristics to create unique styles.

Yeast is the microorganism responsible for fermentation. Contributes to the flavor profile of beer, with different strains creating everything from fruity and spicy notes to clean and crisp finishes.
